Mercury

Mercury is the smallest and innermost planet of our solar system, a rocky, cratered world with no moons. It has the shortest year of any planet, completing an orbit around the Sun in just 88 Earth days, which is why it was named after the swift Roman messenger god. Due to its thin atmosphere, Mercury experiences extreme temperature swings, and despite being closest to the Sun, it isn't the hottest planet.
